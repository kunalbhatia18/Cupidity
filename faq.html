Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I like a person's profile?

Double tap on a profile to like it.

How do I dislike a person's profile?

Click on pass to dislike a person's profile.

Do I have to pay for Cupidity or is it free?

Cupidity, the app and all of its services are absolutely free.

Can I use Cupidity anywhere in the world?

Love has no boundaries. And neither does Cupidity. The app can be used anywhere and anytime in the world.

What is the minimum age requirement?

You have to be at least 18 or above to use Cupidity.

Can I see who I liked on Cupidity?

Unfortunately no, you can't see the profiles you liked earlier.

Can I use Cupidity on my Laptop?

We are really sorry but you can't use Cupidity on laptop.

Can I change my list of favourite artists in app?

Yes. You can always change your favourite artists lists by navigating to Settings -> About -> Favourite artists

Is Cupidity only for straight people?

Love doesn't discriminate and neither do we. With fifty-eight different genders to choose from, there is someone for everyone at cupidity.

Does age filter work both ways?

Yes, the age filter works both ways.

How will I know when I get matched with someone?

If you get matched with someone, while seeing profiles a popup card appears indicating the same. Otherwise, a notification appears so that you know that you have received a match.

What If don’t want to upload a picture of mine?

You can always choose to upload a blank image but people like seeing images to be sure that the other person is genuine.

Do I need a Spotify account to use Cupidity?

We provide you with a list of artists to choose from. Select a minimum of fifteen or a maximum of forty of your favourite artists.

Can I match with someone who does not have same music preferences as me?

Absolutely, if both of you like each other's profiles then it's a match!

Why can’t I start a conversation with somebody I double tapped on?

Both of you must like each other to start a conversation. This is done to prevent spamming.

Contact Us?

If you have any other question do write to us at contact@cupidity.co.in
